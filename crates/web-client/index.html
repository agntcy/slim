SLIM browser MLS moderator

Drives the SessionLayer compiled to wasm32 (via tokio_with_wasm) over a gloo_net WebSocket. The browser is the moderator: it creates an MLS group, invites native participants, and broadcasts end-to-end encrypted messages.
